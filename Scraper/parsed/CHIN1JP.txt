Attention Exhibitors
1. The Fairgrounds will be available to All Breed Show exhibitors at 8:00 am on Friday, March 29,
2013. Dogs must be removed from the show site immediately following Best in Show judging on
Sunday, March 31, 2013.
2. No dogs allowed in buildings between 9:00 pm to 6:00 am. The buildings will be locked during
those hours.
3. No smoking allowed in buildings or within 10 feet of any building entrance or air intake.
4. No ex-pens will be allowed in Main Exhibit Hall, day parking or grooming area in the Santiam
Building. Ex-pens are allowed in the Cascade Livestock Pavilion grooming area.
5. Reserved grooming is available in the Santiam Bldg & Cascade Livestock Pavilion. Unreserved
grooming will be in the Cascade Livestock Bldg may be on a packed surface on a first come first
serve basis after 12:00 Noon, Friday, March 29, 2013. Electricity MAY not be available in unre-
served grooming areas. There will be no grooming ringside, on sidewalks or near building
entrances. Crates allowed at ringside only during judging of the breed.
6. START CLEAN - STAY CLEAN: Handlers & Exhibitors are required to clean up their grooming and
parking areas after the show and to keep it clean during the show. Anyone in violation of this
“Clean Policy Code” will be warned to clean his/her area. Should the violation continue the set-up
may be removed from the grooming area. All grooming areas will be monitored by the PHA &
Grounds Chairman.
7. Should the Show Committee determine that any dog enclosed in a vehicle is in danger, we
reserve the right to break into that vehicle. Anyone parking in the show site parking lots must
accept full responsibility for damages should this occur & waive any claim to damages against
Chintimini Kennel Club, Inc. (the club) or its members.
8. EXTREME WEATHER CONDITIONS: To insure the safety of our dogs, exhibitors & spectators, the
Club has the right to cancel these shows in the event of extreme weather conditions.
9. No drilling nor intensive or corrective training of dogs on the show grounds or premises.
10. No cords across any road or walkway. No generators in or near show buildings or rings. All
extension cords must be heavy duty. Multiplug adaptors and extension cords must meet
code.
11. No unentered dogs allowed in exhibition areas.
12. Exhibitors may videotape/photograph their own animals.
13. Crates may be in the designated area ringside for judging of the breed. Crates must be removed
immediately after judging is completed for that breed.
